**ABU Robocon 2021 Pot** is a dataset for an object detection task. It is used in the robotics industry. 

The dataset consists of 1874 images with 7495 labeled objects belonging to 1 single class (*pot*).

Images in the ABU Robocon 2021 Pot dataset have bounding box annotations. There are 570 (30% of the total) unlabeled images (i.e. without annotations). There are 2 splits in the dataset: *labelled* (1552 images) and *negativeimages_raw* (322 images). Also, the dataset contains ***camera*** tag, *negativeimages_raw* split also contains ***subfolder*** tag. The dataset was released in 2022 by the PolyU FENG Robotics Team.
